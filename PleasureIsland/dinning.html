Restaurants and Dining Options in Carolina Beach and Kure Beach

Pleasure Island is home to some of the area's favorite restaurants, including award winning seafood and locally sourced fare. With options ranging from fresh oysters and peel-and-eat shrimp, to waterfront dining, breakfast diners, take-out, bistros, cafes, and sub shops plus yummy pizza, donuts, ice cream and fudge, Pleasure Island is a popular dining destination for area residents and visitors alike.
